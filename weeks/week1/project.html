Week 1 Project

AI Landscape Analysis

Research and analyze AI applications in your industry of interest. This project will help you connect theoretical knowledge with real-world implementations and identify potential career paths in AI engineering.

Project Overview

This project is designed to help you understand how AI is being applied in real-world scenarios across different industries. By researching specific use cases, you'll gain practical insights into:

  • Current AI technologies being used in business
  • Challenges and opportunities in AI implementation
  • Skills and roles in demand in the AI industry
  • Future trends and potential applications

Why This Project Matters

Understanding real-world AI applications helps bridge the gap between theory and practice. This research will give you context for everything you learn in the following weeks and help you identify potential career paths in AI engineering.

Project Deliverables

1. Industry Research

Choose an industry that interests you and identify AI use cases within it.

  • Select one industry (healthcare, finance, retail, manufacturing, etc.)
  • Research and identify 3 specific AI use cases in this industry
  • For each use case, note: What problem does it solve? What type of AI is used?
  • Find real companies implementing these solutions

2. Deep Dive Analysis

Select one use case for detailed analysis including benefits, challenges, and implementation details.

  • Choose one use case from your research for deeper analysis
  • Research the technical approach (what type of AI/ML is used?)
  • Identify benefits and ROI for the business
  • Note challenges faced during implementation
  • Look for case studies, white papers, or news articles

3. Presentation

Create a comprehensive presentation summarizing your research and insights.

  • Prepare a 5-minute presentation (5-7 slides)
  • Include: Industry overview, 3 use cases, deep dive analysis
  • Add your insights on future opportunities
  • Practice presenting to ensure you stay within time limit

4. Environment Setup

Set up your Python development environment for the hands-on work in upcoming weeks.

  • Install Python 3.8+ on your computer
  • Install Jupyter Notebook or JupyterLab
  • Install key libraries: pandas, numpy, matplotlib, scikit-learn
  • Create a test notebook and run a simple "Hello World" script
  • Verify everything works by running provided test code

Suggested Timeline

Day 1-2

Industry Selection & Initial Research

Choose your industry of interest and begin researching AI applications. Use industry reports, news articles, and company websites to identify potential use cases.

Day 3-4

Deep Dive Analysis

Select one use case for detailed research. Look for technical details, case studies, and implementation challenges. This is where you'll spend the most time.

Day 5

Environment Setup

Install Python and required libraries. Test your setup to ensure everything works properly for upcoming hands-on sessions.

Day 6-7

Presentation Creation & Practice

Create your presentation slides and practice delivering your findings within the time limit. Focus on key insights and takeaways.

Evaluation Criteria

Your project will be evaluated based on:

  • Research Quality (40%): Depth and accuracy of industry and use case research
  • Analysis Depth (30%): Insights and understanding demonstrated in deep dive analysis
  • Presentation (20%): Clarity, organization, and delivery of findings
  • Technical Setup (10%): Successful completion of Python environment setup

Submission Guidelines

Due Date: End of Week 1 (before Week 2 begins)

Format: PDF presentation + screenshot of working Python environment

Length: 5-7 slides maximum for presentation

Submission Checklist

  • Industry research completed (3 use cases identified)
  • Deep dive analysis of one use case
  • Presentation created (5-7 slides)
  • Python environment installed and tested
  • All deliverables ready for submission
