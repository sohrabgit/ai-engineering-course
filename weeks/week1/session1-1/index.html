Introduction to AI & Machine Learning

Welcome to your AI journey! This foundational session introduces you to artificial intelligence and machine learning concepts that will be essential throughout the course. We'll explore what AI really is, how machines learn, and the fundamental approaches that power modern AI systems.

Session Topics

Topic 1 20 minutes

What is Artificial Intelligence?

Start your AI journey by understanding what artificial intelligence really is. We'll demystify AI, explore its different types, and examine real-world applications that you interact with daily.

Learning Objectives

  • Define artificial intelligence in simple terms
  • Distinguish between different types of AI
  • Recognize AI applications in everyday life
  • Understand the current state and future potential of AI
Topic 2 30 minutes

Machine Learning Fundamentals

Dive deep into how machines actually learn. Understand the three types of machine learning, the complete ML workflow, and essential terminology that forms the foundation of modern AI.

Learning Objectives

  • Explain how ML differs from traditional programming
  • Distinguish between supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning
  • Understand the ML workflow from data to predictions
  • Recognize key ML terminology and concepts
Topic 3 20 minutes

Deep Learning Introduction

Explore the neural networks that power modern AI breakthroughs. Learn about deep learning architecture, understand how neural networks mimic the brain, and see real-world applications.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand neural networks and deep learning basics
  • Learn how deep learning differs from traditional ML
  • Explore deep learning applications
  • Prepare for hands-on neural network building

What You'll Achieve This Session

By the end of Session 1.1, you will have:

  • Solid Foundation: Clear understanding of AI vs ML vs Deep Learning
  • Practical Knowledge: Ability to identify which ML approach fits different problems
  • Technical Vocabulary: Comfort with essential AI/ML terminology
  • Real-World Perspective: Recognition of AI applications in daily life and industry
  • Ready for Coding: Conceptual preparation for hands-on implementation in Session 1.2
