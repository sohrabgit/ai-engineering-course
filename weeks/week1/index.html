Week 1

AI Fundamentals & Machine Learning Basics

Begin your AI journey with a comprehensive introduction to artificial intelligence. Understand what AI really is, explore different types of AI systems, and dive into the fundamentals of machine learning that power modern AI applications.

2 Sessions
110 Total Minutes
4-6 Hours This Week

Learning Objectives

By the end of this week, you will be able to:

  • Define artificial intelligence in simple, practical terms
  • Distinguish between different types of AI (Narrow, General, Super AI)
  • Recognize AI applications in everyday life and various industries
  • Understand the current state and future potential of AI technology
  • Explain fundamental machine learning concepts and terminology
  • Set up your Python development environment for AI work

Week 1 Sessions

Session 1.1

What is Artificial Intelligence?

20 minutes
  • Defining AI and its core components
  • Three levels of AI: Narrow, General, and Super AI
  • Real-world AI applications across industries
  • Current capabilities and limitations of AI
Hands-on Activity
AI recognition exercise: Identify AI systems in your daily life and categorize them by type.
Session 1.2

Machine Learning Fundamentals

90 minutes
  • Introduction to Machine Learning concepts
  • Supervised vs Unsupervised vs Reinforcement Learning
  • Data preprocessing and feature engineering basics
  • Model training and evaluation fundamentals
Hands-on Activity
Build your first machine learning model using Python and scikit-learn.
