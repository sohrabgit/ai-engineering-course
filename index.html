Course Overview

Learn by building. Each week combines theoretical foundations with hands-on projects to create real AI systems. From fundamentals to deployment - master the complete AI engineering lifecycle.

Complete AI Stack

From classical ML to modern transformers and LLMs. Master PyTorch, scikit-learn, Hugging Face, and deployment frameworks used in production AI systems.

Weekly Projects

Build real applications: house price predictor, customer churn classifier, image recognition system, NLP applications, and AI agents with tool use.

Production Ready

Learn MLOps, model deployment, API creation with FastAPI, Docker containerization, and building end-to-end AI applications that scale.

6-Week Curriculum

Comprehensive curriculum designed to take you from AI fundamentals to building intelligent systems.

Week 1

AI Fundamentals & Python for AI

Understand what AI, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning are. Set up development environment and master essential Python libraries (NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib). Build your first ML model.

2 Sessions 180 min total
Week 2

Classical Machine Learning

Understand key ML algorithms and when to use them. Master the ML pipeline: preprocessing, training, evaluation. Learn feature engineering and build classification/regression models.

2 Sessions 180 min total
Week 3

Neural Networks & Deep Learning Foundations

Understand how neural networks learn. Build neural networks from scratch and with frameworks. Master PyTorch/TensorFlow basics and train deep learning models effectively.

2 Sessions 180 min total
Week 4

Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs)

Understand how CNNs process images. Build CNN architectures for computer vision. Apply transfer learning and work with pre-trained models for custom image recognition systems.

2 Sessions 180 min total
Week 5

Natural Language Processing (NLP) & Transformers

Understand how to process text data. Learn the Transformer architecture and work with pre-trained language models. Build multi-task NLP applications.

2 Sessions 180 min total
Week 6

LLMs, Agents & Real-World Deployment

Understand Large Language Models and build AI agents that use tools. Deploy models to production and learn MLOps basics. Complete end-to-end AI application project.

2 Sessions 180 min total
